About Us

Welcome to STIR & SIP

The only destination in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar for the freshest fruit juices and aromatic coffee!

Freshness, great taste, and quality you can trust.

STIR & SIP is a modern Juice Bar & Cafe located in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, where we serve fresh fruit juices and aromatic coffee prepared with love and care.

We believe that great taste always comes from freshness. Thats why we use handpicked, fresh fruits every day to create 100% fresh and refreshing fruit juices.

At STIR & SIP, our goal is not just to serve drinks, but to deliver a truly refreshing experience in every sip. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality, hygiene, and customer satisfaction.

For our customers in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, we continuously strive to offer perfect coffee, a clean and welcoming environment, and friendly service.
